Tourly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Tourly ("the App") helps professional tennis players manage their tournament schedule, deadlines, and travel expenses. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to create and sign in to your account.
  • Profile information: name and player details you choose to provide.
  • App content: tournaments, deadlines, and expenses you enter. This data is stored to provide the App's core functionality.
  • Receipt photos: if you use receipt capture, the photo you take or select is sent securely to our server once, only to extract the expense details (amount, merchant, date). The photo itself is not stored after processing.
  • Push notification token: a device token used solely to send you tournament deadline reminders.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: anonymized crash reports and performance data (via Sentry) used to fix bugs and improve stability.

Location

If you grant location permission, your location is used only on your device to center the tournament map on where you are. It is never tracked, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely with our backend provider (Supabase) and transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app, from Settings → Account → Delete Account — deletion is immediate. See our account deletion instructions for details, including what to do if you can't access the app.

Children

Tourly is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Tourly — Delete Your Data

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This page explains how to delete specific data from your Tourly account, or delete your account and all of its data entirely.

Deleting individual data — no need to delete your account

You don't have to delete your whole account to remove specific information. From within the app, at any time:

  • Tournaments: open a tournament and delete it — this also removes its deadlines.
  • Expenses: open an expense entry and delete it.
  • Profile details: edit or clear fields directly in Settings.

These deletions take effect immediately and only remove the specific item you selected — the rest of your account and data is unaffected.

How to delete your entire account

You can delete your Tourly account yourself, directly in the app, in a few taps:

  1. Open Tourly and go to Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately — no waiting period, no need to contact support.

No app access? If you can't sign in to request deletion yourself, email diegoedjarry@gmail.com from the address linked to your account and we will delete it on your behalf within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (name, player details)
  • All tournaments, deadlines, and expenses you entered
  • Your push notification device token

What may be retained

  • Anonymized crash and diagnostic logs (via Sentry) that cannot be tied back to your identity — these are kept for stability analysis and are not linked to your account after deletion.
  • Aggregated, de-identified usage statistics that do not identify you individually.

We do not retain your personal information, account content, or receipt photos beyond the 30-day deletion window.
